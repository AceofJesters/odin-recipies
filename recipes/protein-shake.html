Protein Shake

Ingredients:

  • Banana
  • Milk, any
  • Peanut butter
  • Chocolate sauce
  • Sugar
  • Ice, ideally cubed
  • Sprinkles, ideally rainbow

Preparation:

  1. Put banana, one scoop peanut butter, a generous drizzle of chocolate sauce, and a pinch of sugar in blender
  2. Pour milk into blender until roughly 3/4 of the blenders contents are undermilk
  3. Fill blender the rest of the way with ice cubes
  4. Blend
  5. Test consistency and flavour. Add ice if too watery or other ingredients if opposing flavours are too strong.
  6. Blend until all ingredients are fully integrated
