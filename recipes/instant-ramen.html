Instant Ramen

Ingredients:

  • Instant noodles
  • Flavouring/broth packet (included)
  • Low expectations

Preparation:

  1. Further lower your expectations
  2. Boil water
  3. Place noodle brick in heat-safe container
  4. Pour boiled water over noodle brick until noodle brick is satisfactoraly submerged
  5. Wait however long you feel in your soul is right
  6. Empty contents of flavouring/broth packet into broth
  7. Stir
  8. Do not enjoy (or do, I'm not one to judge (I am, this is a threat))
